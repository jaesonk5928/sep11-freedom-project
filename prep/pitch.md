### MVP Explainations
#### Intro
* Hi, my Freedom Project is about collecting points while avoiding the "fires" that aim to kill you. There are also platforms that are spaced, so you have to time your jumps, or else you lose. (Show the product right after)
#### Purpose
* My purpose of this game to me is that I wanted to make a game that was not too simple & not too complex for an average person. It's a recreation of the original mario game, but less features and more about scoring high.
#### Tool
* My current tool is Phaser, I learned how to use it by using resources like the Phaser website and youtube tutorials. My other backup tool was Three.js, but I figured that making 3D models of sprites were vastly more difficult.
